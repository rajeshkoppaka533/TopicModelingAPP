Each of us has a voice, and a role, in building the company we aspire to be. Two weeks ago, we launched Engagement@Conduent, our first-ever companywide engagement survey, to gather insights into how you feel about working at Conduent. Our goal is simple: to gather your honest and candid feedback around a few key

questions — the answers to which could create meaningful, positive impacts on our ability to make Conduent an employer of choice.
